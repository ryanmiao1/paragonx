## Description

This course is for students who have a solid understanding of Algebra 1 and Geometry and are aiming for a high score on the AMC 8. In this four-week program, we will teach algebra and counting skills necessary to solve AMC 8 problems and the first 15 problems on the AMC 10. In addition, we will give students problem-solving tips specific to math competitions and assign homework on a weekly basis, some of which will be reviewed in class.

## Syllabus

1. Algebra - Systems of Equations, Time/Distance/Work
2. Number Theory - Primes, Prime Factorization
3. Combinatorics - Casework Counting, Complementary Counting, & Constructive Counting
4. Geometry - Triangles
