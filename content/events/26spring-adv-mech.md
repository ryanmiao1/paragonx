## Description

This course is for highly motivated students who want to do USAPhO-level (or other equivalent national Olympiad) questions on classical mechanics. Specifically, this course aims to go beyond the standard AP Physics C: Mechanics curriculum to go into Olympiad-level tricks, ideas, and (most importantly) mindset on how to approach mechanics problems.

Pre-requisites: AP Physics C, AP Calculus BC (or equivalent). Some basic understanding of matrices and partial derivatives are preferred but not required.

## Syllabus

1. Reference Frames and Coordinate Systems
2. Choice of Pivot Point, Complicated Scenarios for Statics Problems
3. Elementary Analysis, Catenary Problem
4. F=dp/dt, Rocket Equation, Product Rule trick for solving ODEs
5. Celestial Mechanics
6. Driven and Damped Oscillations
7. Coupled Oscillations and Normal Modes, Unconventional Scenarios for Oscillation
8. Misc. Topics & The Art of Physics
