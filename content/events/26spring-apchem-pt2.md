## Description

This 12-week class is the second of a 24-class offering (classes 1-12 were offered in the Fall session, and class recordings are available upon request). It will cover the full curriculum on the AP® Chemistry Exam. The class is open to everyone and will begin with the basics of chemistry and cover advanced topics such as electrochemistry and thermodynamics. There is no chemistry knowledge required to take the class.

## Syllabus

1. Acid-Base Equilibria 1
2. Acid-Base Equilibria 2
3. Thermodynamics 1
4. Thermodynamics 2
5. Electrochemistry 1
6. Electrochemistry 2
7. Thermodynamics and Equilibrium Integration
8. Acid Base, Thermodynamics, Electrochemistry Review
9. AP Test Practice
