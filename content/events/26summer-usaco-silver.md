## Description

This four-week course will cover the important topics to know for USACO Silver and is designed for students who are experienced in programming and USACO Bronze topics. Each class will follow the format of lecture mixed with solving and implementing problems. Homework will be assigned after each class, and we will review it at the beginning of the next class.

## Syllabus

1. Prefix Sums, Difference Arrays, Data Structures
2. Searching and Sorting, Two Pointers
3. Greedy Algorithms
4. Graph Algorithms
