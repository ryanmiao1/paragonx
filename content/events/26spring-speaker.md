Speakers from left to right: Joe, Thomas, Arnav, Aarush, Neil 

We will be hosting a speaker event on Sunday, April 12th 2026 with USACO Finalist and USAPHO Gold Medalist Joe Li (Stanford ‘28), USACO Finalist Thomas Liu (MIT '28), USESO Finalist Arnav Swaroop (Berkeley '28), USAMO Gold Medalist Aarush Vailaya (MIT '29), and USAMO Silver Medalist Neil Krishan (MIT '29). These passionate STEM students and former ParagonX officers will share their valuable experience and advice regarding competitions, research, and entrepreneurship. There will also be a Q&A session.
